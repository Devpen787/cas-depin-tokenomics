#### D4. Avoid bureaucratic list prose

Decision:

- avoid `bold label + period + explanation` structures unless the genre truly requires them.

Implication:

1. lists are allowed only when they improve comprehension,
2. tidy formatting must not override teachability,
3. prose should sound chosen rather than auto-completed.

#### D5. Preserve human signals in academic writing

Decision:

- V2 should preserve:
  1. visible judgment,
  2. calibrated uncertainty,
  3. useful repetition where repetition improves clarity,
  4. occasional plainness when plain wording is more accurate than polished wording,
  5. paragraph movement driven by thought rather than by template.

Implication:

1. smoother prose is not automatically better prose,
2. polished-but-empty or auto-completed language should be rejected.
